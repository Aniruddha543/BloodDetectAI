About BloodDetectAI

BloodDetectAI is developed by Aniruddha, a student of the Institute of Engineering & Management (IEM). It blends deep learning with medical imaging to revolutionize blood cell analysis. Using YOLO-based detection and advanced computer vision, the platform efficiently identifies blood cell anomalies. Designed for doctors, laboratories, and researchers, BloodDetectAI automates image detection, reduces diagnostic time, and ensures secure medical data management.
